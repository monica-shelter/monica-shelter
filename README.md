# 🐾 Monica's Shelter — Interactive Map

An interactive map of Monica's dog shelter in Muscelusa, Romania.  
Built for volunteers to quickly find info about each kennel and dog.

---

## What it does

- **Interactive shelter map** — tap any kennel or zone to see the dogs inside, notes, and photos
- **Sidebar** — Daily Routine and Romanian Cheatsheet, always accessible
- **Real-time sync** — any update is visible to all volunteers within seconds (powered by Firebase)
- **Works on phone and computer**
- **Three access levels** to protect the data

---

## Access levels

| Level | What you can do |
|---|---|
| **Read only** | View the map, kennels, dog info, sidebar. No login needed. |
| **Editor** | Everything above + edit kennel notes, dog descriptions, sidebar content. |
| **Master** | Everything above + move/add/delete zones on the map, change passwords, restore backups. |

To log in, tap the **🔒 Log in** button in the top right.

---

## For volunteers

Just open the link. Everything loads automatically.

- **Tap a kennel** → see the dogs inside, notes, and photos
- **☰ MENU** button (left side) → Daily Routine and Romanian Cheatsheet
- **Scroll / pinch** to zoom, **drag** to pan

---

## For editors

Log in with the editor password.

- **Tap any zone** → edit its notes and dog info directly in the card
- **Edit the zone name** in the card header (the title becomes a text field)
- **Save** button inside each card saves to Firebase instantly
- **☰ MENU** → edit Daily Routine and Romanian Cheatsheet

---

## Photo hosting

Photos are stored as **Google Drive share links**.  
To add a photo to a dog:
1. Upload the photo to Google Drive
2. Right-click → **Share** → set to "Anyone with the link"
3. Copy the link (looks like `https://drive.google.com/file/d/XXXX/view`)
4. Paste it into the photo field in the dog's card

The app converts it automatically to a displayable image.
